Each upgrade icon uses the corresponding name listed below:
- Commander
- Officer
- Weapons Team
- Support Team
- Fleet Command
- Fleet Support
- Offensive Retrofit
- Defensive Retrofit
- Experimental Retrofit
- Turbolasers
- Ion Cannons
- Ordinance
- Superweapon
- Title

Commander and title cards display their upgrade icons on their card backs only [^1].
